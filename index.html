what is scratch?

Scratch is a free, visual programming language that allows users to create interactive stories, games, and animations:

Scratch is a block-based coding platform developed by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). It's designed to be user-friendly and accessible to beginners of all ages. Scratch is popular with kids, but it requires basic reading abilities. For younger children, ScratchJr is a simpler approach to programming that's available for free on iOS, Android, and Chromebook.
